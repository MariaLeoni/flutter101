The information we collect


All the information that we collect is based on the information that you provide and your interaction with the app.
- When you log in, you will be required to use your email and phone number, which will be stored for your verification.
- Messages which you send to individuals and groups; and which are sent to you will be saved.
- Your activities such as: publishing posts, liking posts, downloading posts, and commenting on posts will all be saved into a database.

 

How the Data is Used


All data that is collected is used to improve the user experience on the app.
- Your login details are used to ensure your identity and to make the app more safe and secure. Additionally, it is used as a means to contact the user.

For example, if the user has forgotten their password or as been hacked; they will be contacted via the details they provided.
- Messages are stored in order for you to be able to keep a record of messages which you have sent and received.
- Your activities are stored to improve the user experience and display user interaction. As well as to display more posts suited towards your interests.

 

Updates to the Privacy Policy


- Our Privacy Policy is open to change especially as we improve the app. When these changes are made, all users will be informed of this.

You can know whether you are using the current privacy policy by checking the date at the top of the document.
